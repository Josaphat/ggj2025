Welcome to the Rawchester Wiki, OUR guide to Rawchester! This site is updated on a regular basis by Rawchesterians in order to bring you the latest news on the best and worst things in our city. If you're a tourist or a lifetime Rawchestarian yourself, enjoy our pages and always feel free to contribute if you find something new.

Things to Do

  • Gregor Westman Park: A can't-miss for any visitor to Rochester, see the beautiful Jenny River as she flows through the park toward Lake Ontario. Get your map of the park here.
  • Weak Museum of Torture Devices: Are you interested in Brazen Bulls? Iron Maidens? That stretchy thing in the Princess Bride? Well, this is the place for you. See a number of torture devices that are claimed to be medieval but probably just invented by the Victorians! Get your tickets here. Family discounts apply!
  • Private Market: The most secretive place of commerce in Rawchester! Bring your robes and Masonic emblem and maybe, just maybe, you'll get into this place under city hall. Don't know where to go? Just follow the pyramid eye iconography, you won't miss it.
    And there will be no orgies, we definitely don't have orgies here.
